How long does a spray tan last?

How should I prepare for my spray tan?
For the best results, exfoliate your skin 24 hours before your appointment to remove dead skin cells and create a smooth base. Shave or wax at least 12 hours beforehand to allow your pores to settle. On the day of your appointment, arrive with clean, dry skin, free from makeup, deodorant, perfume, and moisturiser, as these can interfere with the tanning solution.
When can I shower after my spray tan?
We recommend waiting 6-8 hours before your first shower. When you do shower, use lukewarm water and avoid using soap on the first rinse. Pat dry gently with a towel.
What should I wear during and after my tan?
During your appointment, wear dark, loose-fitting clothing. After your tan, continue to wear loose, dark clothing until after your first shower to avoid any transfer.
How long does the appointment take?
A typical spray tan appointment takes approximately 30-45 minutes, including preparation and drying time.
